Our Story

Call N Haul was founded on a simple idea — junk removal shouldn't be complicated. Too many companies overcharge, show up late, or leave a mess behind. We set out to change that.

"I started this company because I believe every customer deserves honesty, reliability, and a fair price. One call really should clear it all."

We've completed over 1,000 jobs and earned a 4.9-star rating doing it. Our uniformed crew treats every job — big or small — with the same level of care and respect. When we're done, we don't just haul — we sweep up and leave your space cleaner than we found it.

We're also committed to sustainability. Every load gets sorted for recycling and donation before anything goes to the landfill. It's better for the planet and better for our community.
